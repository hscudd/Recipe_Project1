Georgia Brownies

Made with BEER NUTS Original Peanuts

  • 2 1/2 Squares Unsweetened Chocolate
  • 1/3 Cup Shortening
  • 1 Cup Granulated Sugar
  • 2 Eggs, Well Beaten
  • 1/2 Cup Flour
  • 1 Cup BEER NUTS Original Peanuts
  • 1 TSP Vanilla
  • 2 TBSP Brandy

Preheat the oven to 370 degrees. In a 1-quart saucepan, melt chocolate and shortening together. In a large bowl, add sugar to well beaten eggs. Combine the chocolate and sugar mixtures. Add flour to the mixture. Reserve 16 BEER NUTS; add to mixture along with vanilla and blend well, add brandy; blend well. Spread the dough evenly in a greased square baking pan. Bake for 20 - 25 minutes. Cool slightly, and press reserved BEER NUTS into brownies. When cool cut into sqaures or bars. Enjoy!
